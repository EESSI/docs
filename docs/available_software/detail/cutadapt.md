# cutadapt


Cutadapt finds and removes adapter sequences, primers, poly-A tails and
 other types of unwanted sequence from your high-throughput sequencing reads.

<small>homepage: </small><span class="software-link">[https://opensource.scilifelab.se/projects/cutadapt/](https://opensource.scilifelab.se/projects/cutadapt/)</span>
